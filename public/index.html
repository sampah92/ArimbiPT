Our Profile

PT. Arimbi Putrijaya

Perusahaan Swasta Nasional berbadan hukum, semenjak tahun 1993 telah ikut berperan di Bidang Usaha Jasa Pelaksanaan Konstruksi (Kontraktor). Dengan kapabilitas, pengalaman Perusahaan, didukung oleh Tenaga Ahli dari berbagai Disiplin Ilmu dan dengan penerapan Sistem Manajemen Mutu serta kepercayaan dari para pemberi pekerjaan, telah menjadi modal utama Perusahaan dalam menghasilkan produk pekerjaan yang tepat guna, tepat waktu dan tepat kwalitas, sehingga di dalam perkembangannya telah banyak mendapat kepercayaan untuk mengerjakan berbagai proyek.

Product and Services.

Here is a product description for our company

Asphalt Mixing Plant (AMP)

The asphalt plant is mainly composed of cold aggregate supply system, drum dryer, coal burner, coal feeder, dust collector, hot aggregate elevator, vibrating screen, filler supply system, weighing and mixing system, asphalt storage, bitumen supply system.

Asphalt Pavement

Composite material commonly used to surface roads, parking lots, airports, and the core of embankment dams.

Bridge Construction

Bridge construction is required as it has huge impact on life of people. Social, scientific and technological dimensions for bridge construction must be considered.

Bridge Rehabilitation

Bridge rehabilitation has also become a very essential factor (sometimes even a decisive one) in contemporary bridge engineering.
